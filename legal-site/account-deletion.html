Delete your NearMeU account

You can delete your NearMeU account directly inside the app. Open Settings → Delete Account, complete Google re-authentication, and confirm deletion.

Request deletion without the app

If you no longer have access to the app, send an account deletion request from the Google email address used for NearMeU.

Email an account deletion request

Send the request to {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}. Never send your password, sign-in token, verification code, or private message content.

What to include

  • The Google account email used to sign in to NearMeU.
  • Your NearMeU nickname, if remembered.
  • A clear statement that you want the NearMeU account and associated data deleted.

We may ask you to complete a reasonable ownership-verification step before processing the request. This protects accounts from unauthorized deletion.

What deletion removes

  • Your Firebase Authentication account.
  • Your public and private NearMeU profile.
  • Your NearMeU profile photo stored in Firebase Storage.
  • Exact and approximate location records stored with the profile.
  • Device notification tokens and notification preferences.
  • Block relationships and reports involving your account.
  • User-linked anti-abuse counters, report locks, and moderation audit records.

What may remain

Messages are hidden from the deleted account, but copies of messages may remain visible to the other participant in their conversation. Limited diagnostics or analytics records may remain for a period determined by Firebase product settings, and narrowly required records may be retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Need help?

Contact {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} and mention “NearMeU account deletion” in the subject line.
