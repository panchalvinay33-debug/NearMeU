Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NearMeU collects, uses, stores, shares, and deletes information when you use the NearMeU Android application and related services. NearMeU is intended only for adults aged 18 years or older.

Information we collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive identifiers needed to authenticate your account, including your Firebase user ID and email address.

Profile information. You may provide a nickname, age, gender, who you are looking for, profile photo, and related profile choices. Your nickname, age, gender, looking-for preference, profile photo, approximate location, state/country, online status, and last-seen information may be visible to eligible NearMeU users.

Location information. With your permission, NearMeU receives your device location to provide nearby discovery. Exact coordinates and city are stored in an owner-only private profile. Rounded coordinates and discovery cells are used to find nearby users without exposing your exact location to other users.

Messages and safety content. We store private messages, reply metadata, read/seen status, unsend status, blocks, reports, report reasons, optional report descriptions, and moderation status so the service can provide chat, safety, and abuse-prevention features.

Device and notification information. We store Firebase Cloud Messaging device tokens, platform information, notification preferences, and presence information to deliver notifications and show online status.

Diagnostics and app activity. Production builds may use Firebase Crashlytics, Google Analytics for Firebase, and Firebase Performance Monitoring to collect crash reports, diagnostics, performance measurements, app navigation, and low-cardinality operational events. NearMeU is designed not to place private message text, email addresses, exact coordinates, raw user IDs, tokens, profile names, or photos into custom telemetry fields.

Security signals. Firebase App Check and Google Play Integrity may process app and device integrity signals to help prevent abuse and unauthorized access.

How we use information

  • Authenticate users and maintain accounts.
  • Create profiles and provide nearby discovery.
  • Deliver private messages, replies, read status, and notifications.
  • Apply blocks, reports, rate limits, suspension, and fraud or abuse prevention.
  • Operate account deletion and data cleanup.
  • Diagnose crashes, improve reliability, and measure app performance.
  • Comply with valid legal obligations and protect users, the service, and the public.

How information is shared

With other users. Other eligible users may see the public profile fields described above. Chat participants can see messages sent to them. Reports are not public.

With service providers. NearMeU relies on Google and Firebase services, including Google Sign-In, Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Firebase Cloud Messaging, App Check, Crashlytics, Analytics, and Performance Monitoring. These providers process information on our behalf to operate and secure the service.

For legal and safety reasons. We may disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce our rules, investigate abuse, or protect rights and safety.

NearMeU does not sell personal information and does not currently use personal information for third-party advertising.

Data retention and deletion

We retain account and service data while your account is active and as needed to operate the service. You can delete your account from within the app after re-authentication, or request deletion through our external account deletion page.

Account deletion removes the Firebase Authentication account, public and private profile data, profile photo stored by NearMeU, device notification tokens, block relationships, reports involving the account, and user-linked anti-abuse counters, report locks, and moderation audit records.

Messages are hidden from the deleted account, but copies of messages may remain visible in the other participant's conversation. Diagnostic and analytics records may remain for limited periods according to Firebase product configuration and provider retention controls. Information may also be retained when required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons, and any such retention should be limited to what is necessary.

Your choices

  • Update your profile information in the app.
  • Allow or deny location and notification permissions through Android settings.
  • Turn message notifications or nearby alerts on or off where available.
  • Block or report another user.
  • Unsend eligible messages or delete messages for yourself.
  • Delete your account and associated data.

Security

NearMeU uses authenticated access, Firestore and Storage security controls, Firebase App Check, Play Integrity, server-side validation, rate limits, release signing, encrypted network connections, and restricted backups. No system can guarantee absolute security, so users should avoid sharing highly sensitive information in profiles or messages.

Children

NearMeU is not intended for anyone under 18. Accounts must meet the adult age requirement, and underage use is prohibited.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the service, law, or data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are published.
