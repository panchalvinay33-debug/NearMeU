# Delete your NearMeU account

**Publication draft:** Replace bracketed placeholders before publishing this page on a public HTTPS URL.

NearMeU users can delete their account directly inside the Android app.

## Delete inside the app

1. Open NearMeU.
2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
3. Open **Settings**.
4. Choose **Delete account**.
5. Review the warning and confirm deletion.
6. Complete recent Google reauthentication when requested.

The app starts deletion only after the authenticated user confirms the request. Recent reauthentication may be required to protect the account from unauthorized deletion.

## What the deletion process removes

The deletion workflow attempts to remove:

- the Firebase Authentication account;
- the public user profile;
- owner-only private profile information;
- registered notification devices/tokens;
- user-owned block records;
- reports and other account-associated records covered by the cleanup service;
- other user-owned data defined by the current NearMeU deletion coordinator.

A backend retry process is used when cleanup cannot finish in one attempt.

## Information that may be retained

Some information may be retained when reasonably necessary for:

- fraud, spam and abuse prevention;
- security incident investigation;
- legal obligations and valid legal requests;
- dispute resolution;
- enforcing service restrictions;
- temporary backup, logging or deletion-retry processes.

The final retention periods and legal basis must match the published NearMeU Privacy Policy.

## Cannot access the app?

Send an account-deletion request to **[SUPPORT OR PRIVACY EMAIL]** from the email address associated with your NearMeU account.

Include:

- the subject “NearMeU account deletion request”;
- the account email address;
- enough information for the operator to verify account ownership.

Do not send passwords, verification codes, private chat screenshots or identity documents unless the support team specifically requests an approved verification method.

## Processing time

In-app deletion begins immediately after successful confirmation and reauthentication. Backend cleanup may take additional time where retries are required.
