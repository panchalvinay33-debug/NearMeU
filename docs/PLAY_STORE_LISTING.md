## Product details — English

### App name

`NearMeU`

### Short description — 77/80 characters

`Discover nearby adults and chat privately with location-aware safety controls`

### Full description

NearMeU helps adults discover compatible people nearby and start private conversations.

Create a profile with your nickname, age, gender, who you are looking for, and an optional photo. NearMeU uses your location—with permission—to find relevant profiles in your selected distance range. Your exact coordinates are kept private and are not shown to other users.

Key features:

• Nearby discovery with practical 10 km, 25 km, and 50 km distance options
• Compatibility-based profile discovery
• Private one-to-one text chat
• Replies, seen status, unsend, and delete-for-me controls
• Online and last-seen indicators
• Privacy-safe push notifications that do not include message text
• Block and report tools
• Server-side message rate limits and abuse protection
• Notification and nearby-alert preferences
• In-app account deletion with secure re-authentication

NearMeU is intended only for users aged 18 or older. Users must follow the app's safety rules and respect other people. Do not share passwords, verification codes, financial details, government identifiers, or other highly sensitive information in your profile or chats.

Location privacy:

NearMeU stores exact location information in an owner-only private profile to calculate nearby results. Other users receive only privacy-rounded discovery information, not your exact coordinates.

Account control:

You can block or report users, manage notification preferences, sign out, and delete your account from Settings. A public web resource is also available for account deletion requests when the app is no longer installed.

NearMeU does not currently display third-party advertising or sell personal information.

Internet access, a Google account, and location permission are required for the full nearby discovery experience.
